About Dr. Manojit Sarkar

Dr. Manojit Sarkar is a distinguished medical professional with an extensive background in surgery, specializing in a wide range of disciplines, including Gastroenterology, Hepato-Pancreato-Biliary Surgery, and Oncosurgery. With an MBBS and an MS in Surgery from the prestigious Nil Ratan Sircar Medical College and Hospital (NRSMCH), Kolkata, Dr. Sarkar has built a reputation for his exceptional surgical skills and dedication to patient care.

His academic journey is marked by numerous advanced certifications and fellowships from leading institutions across the globe. Dr. Sarkar has received advanced surgical training from the All India Institute of Medical Sciences (AIIMS), where he completed the Fellowship in Minimal Access Surgery (FMAS). He also holds a Certificate of Competence in Surgery from the Royal College of Surgeons, United Kingdom, further solidifying his expertise in the field. Additionally, he is an Associate Fellow of the American College of Surgeons (aFACS), reflecting his commitment to maintaining the highest standards of surgical practice.

Dr. Sarkar's international credentials are further highlighted by his Diploma in Urology from the United Kingdom and a Postgraduate Diploma in Hepato-Pancreato-Biliary Surgery. His expertise is not limited to these areas; he has also completed fellowships in Gastroenterology, Colo-Proctology, Hernia Surgery, and Breast, Thyroid, and Onco Surgery. These qualifications underscore his comprehensive approach to surgical care, ensuring that his patients receive the most advanced and effective treatments available.

Spectrum of Consultation

Dr. Sarkar's surgical expertise spans multiple divisions, each dedicated to a specific area of the body:

  • Upper GI Division Dr. Sarkar treats conditions affecting the esophagus, such as dysphagia, GERD, and esophageal tumors. His stomach-related treatments include managing gastritis, gastric ulcers, and gastric cancer, providing both medical and surgical solutions.
  • Intestine Division He addresses a range of small intestine diseases and tumors, as well as large intestine disorders, including colon cancer. His approach to appendicitis is marked by specialized surgical interventions.
  • Hepato-Pancreato-Biliary & Splenic Division Dr. Sarkar offers comprehensive care for gallbladder and bile duct stones, pancreatitis, pancreatic tumors, and liver infections. His surgical expertise also extends to treating splenic tumors and splenomegaly.
  • Proctology Division In this division, Dr. Sarkar specializes in the surgical treatment of piles, fissures, and fistulas, providing relief from these often painful conditions.
  • Endocrine Division Dr. Sarkar manages thyroid disorders, breast tumors, parathyroid and adrenal gland tumors, with a focus on surgical treatment when necessary.
  • Hernia Division His hernia care includes the treatment of inguinal hernias, incisional hernias, and ventral hernias, with a particular emphasis on laparoscopic surgical techniques.
  • Urology Division Dr. Sarkar is proficient in the surgical management of kidney stones, bladder diseases, prostate conditions, and testicular tumors, offering advanced surgical solutions for these urological issues.
  • Retroperitoneum Division This division focuses on the diagnosis and surgical treatment of retroperitoneal tumors, ensuring that patients receive targeted care for these complex conditions.
  • Vascular Division Dr. Sarkar provides specialized care for varicose veins, offering surgical interventions to alleviate symptoms and prevent complications.

His work has been published in reputable medical journals, reflecting his ongoing commitment to the advancement of medical knowledge and practice.
