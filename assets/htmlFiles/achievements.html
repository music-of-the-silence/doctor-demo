Dr. Sarkar's Achievements

Dr. Manojit Sarkar holds an MBBS and MS in Surgery from NRSMCH, with advanced surgical training from premier institutions such as AIIMS (FMAS) and New Delhi (CMAS). He is also an Associate Fellow of the American College of Surgeons (aFACS) and has completed a Certificate of Competence in Surgery from the Royal College of Surgeons. His international credentials include a Diploma in Urology from the UK and a Postgraduate Diploma in Hepato-Pancreato-Biliary (HPB) Surgery. Additionally, he has completed a Fellowship in Gastroenterology, along with certificates in Gastroenterology and Hepatology, Colo-Proctology, Hernia Surgery, and Breast, Thyroid, and Onco Surgery.

Special Interests

The doctor has a keen interest in Upper Gastrointestinal (GI) Surgery, Oncosurgery, and Urosurgery, which form the core of his specialized practice.

Professional Role

He serves as a Consultant Laparoscopic Surgeon with expertise in Gastrointestinal, Colorectal, Oncological, and General Surgery, providing comprehensive care across these disciplines.

Spectrum of Consultation

In the 'Upper GI Division', the doctor addresses conditions affecting the esophagus, such as dysphagia, GERD, and esophageal tumors. His expertise extends to the stomach, where he treats gastritis, gastric ulcers, and gastric cancer.

Within the 'Intestine Division', he manages a range of small intestine diseases and tumors, offering surgical solutions when necessary. His services also include large intestine disease management and colon cancer surgery. For the appendix, he provides specialized treatment for appendicitis.

The 'Hepato-Pancreato-Biliary & Splenic Division' covers a wide array of conditions, including gallbladder and bile duct stones, for which he offers both symptomatic treatment and surgery. He also treats pancreatitis, pancreatic tumors, and pancreatic stones, with a focus on surgical intervention. His liver expertise includes managing infections, fatty liver, and tumors, while in the spleen, he treats tumors and splenomegaly through surgery.

In the 'Proctology Division', the doctor offers treatment for piles, as well as fissures and fistulas, with a focus on surgical care when required.

The 'Endocrine Division' includes comprehensive care for thyroid disorders, where he manages both symptoms and surgical treatment. He also specializes in treating breast tumors and cancer, as well as parathyroid and adrenal gland tumors, utilizing surgery as needed.

For hernia-related issues, the 'Hernia Division' provides specialized care for inguinal hernias, as well as incisional and ventral hernias, with a focus on surgical solutions.

In the 'Urology Division', the doctor treats kidney stones and tumors, ureteric stones, bladder diseases, and stones, with surgical interventions available as needed. He also manages prostate diseases and tumors, hydroceles, and testicular tumors through surgery.

The 'Retroperitoneum Division' focuses on the diagnosis and surgical treatment of retroperitoneal tumors.

Finally, in the 'Vascular Division', the doctor provides surgical care for varicose veins, addressing both symptoms and complications.
