Innovation & research

Dr. Manojit Sarkar co-authored the study "PROM and Its Maternal Outcome: A Retrospective Study in a Rural Medical College of India," published in the International Journal of Reproduction, Contraception, Obstetrics and Gynecology (May 2021). This research investigates the incidence and management of Premature Rupture of Membranes (PROM) in a rural setting, highlighting its effects on maternal and neonatal outcomes. The study analyzed data from 545 cases, emphasizing the importance of timely and appropriate management of PROM to minimize risks and improve outcomes.
